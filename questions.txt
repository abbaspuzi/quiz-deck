1. You see a bouquet with a Purple wrapper on the line. Which product is it?
   a) Laycie
   b) Trixie
   c) Mischa
   d) Miriam
Answer: Trixie
Explanation: Trixie is the only Mother's Day product with a purple exterior.

2. A bouquet features a cream wrapper with 3 pink rubber ribbons attached directly to the paper. Which product is this?
   a) Trixie
   b) Mischa
   c) Laycie
   d) Miriam
Answer: Laycie
Explanation: The 3-ribbon count on the cream wrap is a unique technical identifier for Laycie.

3. Which Russian-style bouquet uses a yellow wrapper and a white ribbon?
   a) Marilyn
   b) Miriam
   c) Mischa
   d) Laycie
Answer: Miriam
Explanation: Miriam = Yellow/White wrapper. Marilyn = Pink/Black wrapper.

4. A Marilyn LARGE bouquet should have how many Pink Gerberas?
   a) 3 Gerberas
   b) 5 Gerberas
   c) 4 Gerberas
   d) 6 Gerberas
Answer: 5 Gerberas
Explanation: Marilyn: Medium (M) = 3 stems; Large (L) = 5 stems.

5. If a Zooey bouquet contains only 3 Sunflowers, which size should be marked on the order tag?
   a) Medium (M)
   b) Large (L)
Answer: Medium (M)
Explanation: 3 sunflowers = Medium; 5 sunflowers = Large.

6. For a Beatrice LUXE order, what is the required hardware and hero lily count?

Explanation: Standard = 3 Lilies in a Standard box; Luxe = 5 Lilies in an XL box.

10. A runner brings a Peach Hatbox to the QC station. It contains Greenheart Sunflowers and Red Rose Spray. Which product is this?
   a) Zooey Box
   b) Zendaya Box
Answer: Zendaya Box

11. You are auditing a Marilyn Russian bouquet. You notice White Matthiola has been added as a filler. Is this correct?
   a) Yes, it is correct
   b) No, it should not be there
Answer: No, it should not be there
Explanation: Marilyn is specifically characterized by the absence of White Matthiola. If Matthiola is present, it is likely a Miriam or an assembly error.

12. A packer has two peach hatboxes. Box A has 3 Anthuriums and Lilac Spidermums. Box B has 1 Anthurium and Pink Gerberas. What is Box A?
   a) Zaylee
   b) Ayame
   c) Zandra
   d) Tessa
Answer: Zaylee
Explanation: The Anthurium count (3 vs 1) is the decisive factor. 3 Anthuriums + Spidermums = Zaylee; 1 Anthurium + Gerberas = Ayame.

13. A packer has two peach hatboxes. Box A has 3 Anthuriums and Lilac Spidermums. Box B has 1 Anthurium and Pink Gerberas. What is Box B?
   a) Ayame
   b) Zaylee

Explanation: Box B with 1 Anthurium and Pink Gerberas = Ayame. Zaylee uses 2–3 Anthuriums and has 0 Gerberas.

14. Look at the photo. Is this an Ayame or a Zaylee?
