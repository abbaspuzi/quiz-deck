1. You see a bouquet with a Purple wrapper on the line. Which product is it?
   a) Laycie
   b) Trixie
   c) Mischa
   d) Miriam
Answer: Trixie
Explanation: Trixie is the only Mother's Day product with a purple exterior.

2. A bouquet features a cream wrapper with 3 pink rubber ribbons, and a white cut orchid. Which product is this?
   a) Trixie
   b) Mischa
   c) Laycie
   d) Miriam
Answer: Laycie
Explanation: The 3-ribbon count on the cream wrap & white cut orchid is a unique technical identifier for Laycie.

3. Which Russian-style bouquet uses a yellow wrapper and a white ribbon?
   a) Marilyn
   b) Miriam
   c) Mischa
   d) Laycie
Answer: Miriam
Explanation: Miriam = Yellow/White wrapper. Marilyn = Pink/Black wrapper.

4. A Marilyn LARGE bouquet should have how many Pink Gerberas?
   a) 3 Gerberas
   b) 5 Gerberas

Answer: 5 Gerberas
Explanation: Marilyn: Medium (M) = 3 stems; Large (L) = 5 stems.

5. If a Zooey bouquet contains only 3 Sunflowers, which size should be marked on the order tag?

7. What size is this Zaylee Pink Mum Box?

8. You are auditing a Marilyn Russian bouquet. You notice White Matthiola has been added as a filler. Is this correct?
   a) Yes, it is correct
   b) No, it should not be there
Answer: No, it should not be there
Explanation: The Marilyn Russian Bouquet is the one without White Matthiola. If Matthiola is present, it is likely a Miriam or an assembly error.

9. A packer has two peach hatboxes. Box A has 3 Anthuriums and Lilac Spidermums. Box B has 1 Anthurium and Pink Gerberas. What is Box A?
   a) Zaylee
   b) Ayame
   c) Zandra
   d) Tessa
Answer: Zaylee
Explanation: The Anthurium count (3 vs 1) is the decisive factor. 3 Anthuriums + Spidermums = Zaylee; 1 Anthurium + Gerberas = Ayame.

19. You are auditing an orchid pot. It has three stalks, but all of them are pink, and it is in a plain white pot with no decorative wrapping. Is this an Indira?
    a) Yes, it is an Indira
    b) No, it is not an Indira
Answer: No, it is not an Indira
Explanation: An Indira must have a mix of 2 pink and 1 white stalks AND the pink decorative wrap around the pot. A 3-stalk pink-only arrangement in a plain pot would be a production error.
